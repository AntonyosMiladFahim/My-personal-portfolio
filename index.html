Hello, I'm Antonyos Milad

Creative

From a young age, I’ve been driven by curiosity — the kind that makes you take things apart just to see how they work. My passion for technology began the day my father brought home our first touchscreen phone. I was fascinated, asking him if the screen could really feel me. That spark turned into a lifelong drive to understand, build, and push technology further. In high school, I pushed beyond limits — from over 100,000 students, I earned top results that led me to Alexandria University’s Faculty of Engineering. There, I achieved a 4.0 GPA and ranked #1 out of 1,200 students, earning a place in the most competitive department: Computer and Systems Engineering. Today, I channel that same passion into building scalable, responsive, and performant solutions that integrate seamlessly with complex systems. I don’t just design interfaces — I engineer them. With an investigative character and love for starting from zero, I turn complexity into elegance. Passionate, relentless, and always seeking growth, I’m ready to contribute to impactful projects and shape the future of technology.
